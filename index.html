    Neha Digital Photo studio is co-founded by passionate artist and photographers with a vision to revolutionize commercial photography. Satish Kumawat saw an opportunity to leverage his background in Art field.At Neha Digital Photo studio ,our primary objective is to provide superior quality functions, commercial, interior, table tops, fine arts, industrial fashion and 360 degree photography.     About My Device

    About My Device

    The all-new D800 digital SLR camera will turn your imagination into stunning still images and spectacular videos. With its revolutionary 36.3 megapixels and the EXPEED3 image-processing engine, the D800 is your ideal professional camera.

    Designed for versatility and high performance, this D-SLR is equipped to handle massive amounts of data with ultra-fast speed. D800 also offers superior image quality and supports ISO sensitivities from 50 to 25600. In addition, videos can be filmed in FX- and DX-based movie formats, creating true cinematic experiences.

    For those who are seeking extraordinary details in their images, the D800E was specially built to create the sharpest shots by disabling the aliasing and moiré pattern reduction operation performed by the optical low-pass filter

    Key Features :

    • Wide sensitivity range of ISO 100 to 6400, expandable to Lo 1 (ISO 50 equivalent) or Hi 2 (ISO 25600 equivalent).
    • Further improved high-speed, multifunctional, high-performance image-processing engine EXPEED 3
    • Continuous shooting at approx. 4 fps* (FX, 5:4) and approx. 5 fps* (1.2x, DX). 　With EN-EL15 Rechargeable Li-ion Battery.
