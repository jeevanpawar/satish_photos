  • My Bio

    Satish Kumawat


    Neha Digital Photo studio is co-founded by passionate artist and photographers with a vision to revolutionize commercial photography. Satish Kumawat saw an opportunity to leverage his background in Art field.At Neha Digital Photo studio ,our primary objective is to provide superior quality functions, commercial, interior, table tops, fine arts, industrial fashion and 360 degree photography. We believe whole heartedly that every client and every work by extension is unique. We always work to satisfy our client.

    Because of this strongly held belief, we take time to understand our clients vision for their day and their unique style. We then use that information to match clients with one of our extra ordinary photographers.
